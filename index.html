About Me

Every great life begins with an even greater story.

Hello, I'm Scott, and welcome to my official website! If you've wondered, "Who is Scott?"—you're in the right place to find out. Grab your favorite beer, take a seat, and let's connect.



Growing up as the only child in a military family in Monkeybay, shaped by my father's service as a marine, I developed into a total introvert. In 2014, a friend named Samuel Yute introduced me to modern tech during my brief stint at UNIMA. He sparked my passion for web development, teaching me HTML, CSS, and JavaScript through tutorials by Bucky Roberts—an unforgettable turning point.



Throughout college, I dabbled in software development, earning a reputation among friends as a coding enthusiast. After navigating through three jobs, each left behind in pursuit of a different lifestyle, I found my niche in technical writing, with web development as a cherished hobby.



Astrophysics and Cosmology are my other passions. The mysteries of black holes and distant galaxies fascinate me deeply, drawing inspiration from figures like Neil deGrasse Tyson, Michio Kaku, Brian Cox, and Brian Green.



Today, I continue to explore the intersection of technology and creativity, embracing a path less traveled to carve out a fulfilling career and life journey.
